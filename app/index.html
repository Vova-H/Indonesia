Local kitchen
What to try
The population of Indonesia is scattered across the islands, which affected the peculiarities of the national cuisine. Each island has its own culinary traditions and preferences.

Indonesian cuisine is famous for its variety of spices: cardamom, ginger, turmeric, nutmeg, sage, cinnamon.

The locals drink plenty of food with fruit cocktails, tea, coconut milk and a drink made from sugar cane.
