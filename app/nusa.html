Nusa Da and Ubud city
Tours to the island of Bali are always at the peak of popularity among tourists and especially romantics! Their not passing charm characterizes the secret of the "island of the gods" as a whole. This is a successful synthesis of the best parameters - the developed infrastructure of the island, its high civilization, unusual culture and religion, the friendliness and openness of local residents, the natural beauty of its various corners, an excellent hotel base and the highest level of service. Bali's beaches, from black to golden, are not as crazy as the beaches of the Maldives or the Dominican Republic. But beach and romantic tours to Indonesia are incredibly popular also because the rainy season on the eastern islands is weak, there are rarely storms or typhoons. The weather in Bali is comfortable and without sharp temperature fluctuations, so the island functions as a beach destination all year round.
